INTRODUCTION

Over view of Es Higiro's History

ES HIGIRO was founded in year of 1980 and from then up to now it has been excellent in delivering quality courses from CELAI to GENERAL EDUCATION. ES HIGIRO was founded by Rwandan governomment in GISAGARA district, NYANZA sector, HIGIRO cell exactly in NYARUTEJA CELL. ES HIGIRO welcomes all students from all backgrounds, allowing them to fetch knowledge together.
