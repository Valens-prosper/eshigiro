Rwandan Education System

Secondary Education

Lower Secondary (O-Level): 3 years (S1 to S3)

General subjects with a focus on foundational skills in sciences, arts, and technical subjects.

Upper Secondary (A-Level): 3 years (S4 to S6)

Specialization in combinations, such as:

  • MCB: Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology
  • MEG: Mathematics, Economics, Geography
  • LFK: Literature, Kinyarwanda, Kiswahili

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

Introduced in 2016 to replace the knowledge-based curriculum.

Focus:

  • Critical thinking
  • Creativity
  • Communication
  • Entrepreneurship

Continuous assessment is integrated with national exams.

TVET Education

Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET):

TVET schools provide skills-oriented training for practical careers, such as:

  • Construction
  • Electricity
